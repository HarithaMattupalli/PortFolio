Social Media

Pinterest

Pinterest is a social media web and mobile application company.The site was founded by Ben Silbermann. The company inspires users to "go out and do that thing."

Whatsapp

WhatsApp Messenger is a freeware, cross-platform messaging and Voice over IP (VoIP) service owned by Facebook. It allows the sending of text messages.

Facebook

Facebook is an American online social media and social networking service company based in Menlo Park, California. It was founded by Mark Zuckerberg.

Snapchat

Snapchat is a multimedia messaging app used globally,created by Evan Spiegel. In Snapchat pictures and messages are usually available for a short time.
